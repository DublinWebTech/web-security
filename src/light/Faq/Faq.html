How to keep your devices safe?

Never download apps from untrusted sources. Always check the permissions of the app before downloading it.
Use an anti virus with a password manager to keep your passwords safe also you can use a VPN to keep your data safe.
And always update your devices and apps to the last versions.

How to be aware of phishing/ smishing/ scam ?

Be aware of everything you receive if it's not registered on your device, even if it seems to be from a friend or a family member. If you are not sure, ask them if they sent you this to be sure.
Be careful with the links you click on, if you are not sure, don't click on it or use tools to authentify it.
You can see if there is a lot of mistakes in the text, if it's not the case, it's probably a scam because real organisations are really careful about this.
Also never give any personal informations such as your bank account number, your address, your phone number, your email, your password, etc.
